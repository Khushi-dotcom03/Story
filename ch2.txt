Chapter 2: The Push  
----------------------  

The commit was made, but the journey was far from over.  
The repository stood tall, waiting for the next command.  

The coder hesitated. What if something went wrong?  
What if the push was rejected?  

But hesitation was the enemy of progress.  
Fingers hovered over the keyboard, then struck with purpose:  

git push origin main  

The request was sent. The repository would decide its fate.
